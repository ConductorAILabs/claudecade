DESIGN NOTES:
- All letters use box-drawing border characters (╔ ╗ ╚ ╝ ║ ═)
- Interior fill uses █ (full block) for solid areas
- Each letter is exactly 6 lines tall
- Consistent width per letter (pad with spaces)
- Professional arcade/terminal aesthetic
- Compatible with CLAUDECADE design style
- Readable and bold while remaining compact
